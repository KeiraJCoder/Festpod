meet your hosts

eamon shortt

Eamon believes festivals are more than just music events. They are his second home, a retreat from the chaos of the world. His love for festivals has shaped his life, from making lifelong friends to finding his soulmate. Join him as he shares the magic, the community, and the mental health benefits of the festival experience.

rich holmes

Rich is an avid supporter of Wrexham AFC, embodying the spirit of the club. Beyond football, his infectious enthusiasm extends to the world of professional wrestling. As a co-presenter of Festpod, Rich brings his vibrant personality, witty banter, and extensive knowledge to create an engaging and entertaining podcast for football, wrestling, and festival fans alike.

neil wilkinson

Neill is a passionate lover of TV and radio broadcasting. With a vast collection of nostalgic VHS tapes containing hours of 90s TV shows, he's on a mission to upload and enhance these recordings for the world to enjoy. His expertise extends to hosting podcasts and online streams, where he shares his love for the medium.

leigh ellingham

Leigh is an enthusiastic rock and metal lover who found his passion for festivals through his love for music. Co-hosting Festpod with Neil, he aims to guide and connect festival-goers, whether they are first-timers or seasoned veterans. Join him to keep the excitement of festivals alive all year round.
